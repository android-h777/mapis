A. Intended Application & Industry Review (Review to be completed prior to NPI GO)
1. What is the intended use or application? What industry sector?
2. Can the product get used in civil and military application? In case of military application, what specific application can it get used for?
3. Is the product specially designed for military use (SDFMU)?
• Yes or No (If yes, you need to consult with GTC prior next steps. Contact your regional GTC partner)
4. Can the material get used in the upstream exploration and production process in O&G industry?
• Yes or No (If yes, you need to consult with GTC prior next steps. Contact your regional GTC partner)
5. Is the product getting developed exclusively for a new customer not having any other business yet?
• Yes or No (If the answer is YES, you need to consult with GTC to have an ad-hoc screen of the business partner performed in EOD)
B. Product Formulation Review (To be completed before Lab Formulation Review, review and Update before Commercial Go)
6. Check substance content as part of considered formulations against restricted chemical substances subject to Import and/or Export controls, e.g. CWC, PIC, Drug Precursors, etc. (The full CAS number reference list can get obtained here: LINK)
C. Proposed Supply Chain Review (To be completed before Scale-up and review/update before Comm. Go)
7. Which raw materials are needed and what potential sourcing options exist?
(You may want to consult with the responsible Procurement Commodity Manager)
8. What are the potential MPM plants to produce this product?
9. What are the key geographical markets targetted?
10. What is the anticipated manufacturing process (Chemical Rule). Please refer to guidance on "Chemical Rules" here
